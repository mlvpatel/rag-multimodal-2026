Acme Corporation Employee Handbook (excerpt).

Paid time off. New employees receive 15 days of paid vacation per year. After five years of service this increases to 25 days. Vacation must be requested at least two weeks in advance through the HR portal.

Remote work. Employees may work remotely up to three days per week. Fully remote arrangements require both manager and HR approval and are reviewed every six months.

Sick leave. Employees receive 10 paid sick days per year, separate from vacation. A doctor's note is required for any absence longer than three consecutive days.

Expenses. Business expenses under 100 dollars can be reimbursed without pre approval. Expenses over 100 dollars require manager approval before the purchase. Reimbursement requests must be submitted within 30 days and must include a receipt.
